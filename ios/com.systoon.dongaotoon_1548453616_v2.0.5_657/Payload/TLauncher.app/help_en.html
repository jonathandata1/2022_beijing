What's the difference between MsgSeal and traditional mail?

We make the mailbox have a series of capabilities such as instant messaging, group, audio and video calling, etc. However, due to security reasons, MsgSeal is not compatible with traditional mailboxes such as gmail, 163, QQ, etc. for binding and access, but it can communicate with all mailboxes normally.
