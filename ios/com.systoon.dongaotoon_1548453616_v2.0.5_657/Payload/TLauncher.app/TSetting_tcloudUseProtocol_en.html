Article 1 Definition of a Crypto Passport

A crypto passport is a special digital identification that is securely kept and used by you to prevent your identity from being stolen and to safely and quickly identify you. In other words, a crypto passport is the only valid credential that identifies you.

Article 2 Generation of a Crypto Passport

When you first use the MsgSeal client, a crypto passport will be automatically generated for identifying you.
